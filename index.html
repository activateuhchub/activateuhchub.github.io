Activate your UCard — quickly & securely

Activate your UCard quickly and securely with a simple online process. Just enter your card details, confirm your identity, and complete activation in minutes. Once activated, you can check benefits, view balances, and start using your card right away. Fast, safe, and designed to get you ready to use your UCard without any hassle.

Activate your UCard (step-by-step)

Before you start: have your 17-digit card number (printed under the barcode on back of card) and personal info that matches your member record.

  1. 1
    Go to the activation website

    Open your browser and visit activate.uhc.com. If your card mentions CareFlex, use the CareFlex activation URL shown on the card materials.

  2. 2
    Enter your card details

    Type the full card number exactly as printed. Double-check digits — mistakes will prevent activation.

  3. 3
    Create or sign in to your account

    You may be asked to sign in with your UnitedHealthcare account (HealthSafe ID or member account). Follow on-screen prompts to create an account if needed.

  4. 4
    Confirm & finish

    Review the details and submit. You should see a confirmation message and may receive an email. If activation fails, try the troubleshooting tips below.

How activation works ?

Activation links your UCard to your UnitedHealthcare member account so the system can verify your identity and load your eligible benefits. Once the activation process is complete, you can instantly access balances, shop with your benefits, and manage everything through the UCard Hub or UHC app.

Where you can use the UCard

  • Use your UCard at participating retail stores like Walmart, Walgreens, and CVS (depends on your plan).
  • Some plans allow use for eligible utilities, OTC items, and healthy foods.
  • Use it online through the UCard Hub to shop eligible items.
  • Use it in the UnitedHealthcare mobile app to manage benefits.

Security & privacy

UnitedHealthcare protects your information with secure sign-in and encrypted systems, so always activate your UCard only through trusted UHC websites. Never share your card number or personal details through email, text, or unknown links. Always check the URL and sign out when using shared devices to keep your account safe.

Frequently asked questions

Q: I don’t see my balance after activation — what do I do?

A: Wait a few minutes and refresh. If still not showing, sign out and sign back in. If the problem persists, contact member services — the number is printed on the back of your UCard or available on uhc.com.

Q: The site says my card number is invalid.

A: Confirm you entered all digits without spaces, check for 0 vs O, and ensure you're using the activation URL printed for your card. If you recently received the card, it can take up to 24–48 hours for systems to fully provision new cards in some scenarios.

Q: Can I activate by phone?

A: Yes — many UCard materials include a toll-free activation phone line; call the number on the back of your card or your plan materials.
